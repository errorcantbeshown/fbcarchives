Bright Falls (AWE-62)

DETAILS:

In conjuction with the previously established monitoring station at Cauldron Lake, the Bright Falls Sheriff Station served as a temporary field office for the Bureau during the aftermath of the latest AWE. In a suspected panic, Bureau agent Erik Antbishone—breaking all proper protocols—shipped an unknown number of classified documents and/or items to Bureau Director Miro via the United States Postal Service. Upon discovery, he was detained by Agent Kiran Estevez held in the a Sheriff Station jail cell for the remainder of the cleanup at Bright Falls and its surrounding areas.
